# Step 1: Create a Developer Account

Step 1: Create a Developer Account
Before you can create or publish a Marketplace app, you need access to the
Marketplace Developer Portal
. This is where you manage your apps, team access, OAuth settings, and submission workflow.
Here’s how to create your Developer Account:
Go to the
HighLevel App Marketplace
Click
Sign Up
to create a developer account.
Fill in the Sign Up form with the details requested.
Verify your Phone Number.
Click Get Started to create your account.
Check your inbox for the verification email and complete email verification.
Ownership and team access (important)
​
The
first user
who signs up becomes the
Owner
of the developer account.
There can be
only one Owner
.
If you need multiple people to access the Developer Portal, add them as users (recommended) instead of sharing credentials.

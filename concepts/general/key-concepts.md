# Key Concepts

Key Concepts
Before you build and submit your first HighLevel App Marketplace app, it’s important to understand how the HighLevel ecosystem is structured and how Marketplace apps are installed and used. HighLevel is primarily used by agencies who manage multiple sub-accounts (locations) for their clients—often under the agency’s own white-labeled brand—so “who installs” and “who uses” your app can vary by setup.
